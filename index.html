About Us

We're a team of college freshmen passionate about revolutionizing off-campus living. Recognizing that everyone has unique housing needs, we're dedicated to offering personalized apartment rankings, moving away from the 'one size fits all' mindset. As freshmen ourselves, we're intimately familiar with the challenges and inconveniences of finding the right place, and we're committed to making this process easier for students like us. Our mission is to "Amplify your living experience in the hidden gem of an apartment that's perfectly suited for you."
